This is an article on how to flash unsupported apps to your device. (e.g. Weather/Stocks/Phone/Passbook on iPad, or PhotoBooth/Facetime on iPhone.)

This tutorial has been tested for iOS 6. It might work on other versions as well.
ONLY WORKS ON INTEL MACS. iDecrypt has not worked in my testing, but you can try it.
Downloads for Mac and Windows are hosted on my server.

========PART 1: UNZIPPING IPSW========
1. Download IPSW. Check Here!
2. With your IPSW, change the extension to .ZIP & open it w/ WinRAR or 7zip.
3. You'll see 3 .DMG files. Find the biggest one in size & extract it.

========PART 1.5: IDECRYPT ROUTE: (Windows or MacOS)========
1a. Download iDecrypt
2a. Visit this link to get your Firmware Key.
3a. Click on your IPSW's iOS version (mine is 6.x)
4a. Find your IPSW's device model & specific iOS version (e.g. iPad2,1 / iOS 6.1.3)
5a. Copy ROOTFS key.
6a. Open iDecrypt & select the DMG you extracted earlier
7a. Paste in the key.
8a. Select destination path
9a. Decrypt.

========PART 1.5: DMGtool ROUTE: (MacOS, Intel-specific)========
1b. Download DMGtool & save it to your desktop. Rename it from DMG to DMGtool to save confusion.
Reddit thread, Original download, Local download (hosted by me)
2b. Visit this link to get your Firmware Key.
3b. Click on your IPSW's iOS version (mine is 6.x)
4b. Find your IPSW's device model & specific iOS version (e.g. iPad2,1 / iOS 6.1.3)
5b. Copy ROOTFS key.
6b. Open Terminal on your Mac.
7b. CD to the directory of the DMGtool file.
8b. "chmod 755 /user/username/Desktop/dmgtool"
9b. The icon should change from a white sheet to a terminal.
10b. Have your extracted DMG, key, and DMGtool ready.
Make sure that your extracted DMG & DMGtool are in the same directory.
11b. Enter the command: "dmgtool extract (replace w/ filename of extracted DMG).dmg decrypted_rootfs.dmg -k (your key)"
E.g. "dmg extract 058-24110-023.dmg output.dmg -k 7f68bec2637d5ac0837e38878a8a411c72131fc6394b5a84fd0f878f54f97af3801f34fd"
If everything goes well, you should have an unencrypted filesystem
(decrypted_rootfs.dmg) that you can browse freely.
